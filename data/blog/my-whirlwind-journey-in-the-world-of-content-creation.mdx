My content creation journey took a turn when I got hooked on Escape From Tarkov. I decided to start streaming the game, and you won't believe it; it actually made me some money! ## The COVID Connection: üåê

Just when I thought my content creation journey couldn't get any crazier, COVID-19 hit. Suddenly, content creation became my lifeline to the outside world. It was like a virtual escape, connecting me to people when I couldn't do so in person. Gaming was not only a source of fun but also a way to stay sane during lockdowns!

## My Team Player Phase: üéÆ

During my content creation escapades, I crossed paths with ThatFriendlyGuy when he was starting his streaming journey. I joined his moderation team, which was a blast. Eventually, I agreed to edit his content and got to work on some seriously fun stuff. Imagine trying to play Escape From Tarkov as a pacifist, that was one wild ride!

## Collabs Galore: ü§ù

But wait, there's more! I also found myself working with BritishUbba to bring his content to YouTube and TikTok. Plus, I edited a season trailer video for a tournament called FaceOff, hosted by ShiftyTTV. Oh, and let's not forget the montage video I made for DoubleDstroyer, a super awesome content creator! Collaborations galore, and it was an absolute blast.

So there you have it, my rollercoaster ride through content creation. From Minecraft dreams to pandemic-inspired connections, it's was a wild journey. I'm cherishing every memory and looking back on all the fun I've had with a smile. Content creation, you've been a real hoot! üöÄüéÆüéâ
